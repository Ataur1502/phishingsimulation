Stay Ahead: Identify the Different Types of Phishing Attacks!

Learn to recognize various phishing attacks and protect your sensitive information from cyber threats.

  • Email Phishing

    Attackers send fraudulent emails that appear to come from reputable sources to trick recipients into providing personal information or clicking malicious links.

    Know More

  • Spear Phishing

    A targeted form of phishing where attackers personalize their messages to specific individuals or organizations, often using information gathered from social media.

    Know More

  • Whaling

    This type of phishing targets high-profile individuals, such as key decision-makers, often involving highly personalized messages to steal sensitive information.

    Know More

  • Smishing and Vishing

    Smishing refers to phishing via SMS text messages, while vishing involves voice calls. Both aim to extract personal information by impersonating legitimate individuals.
